Q: Difference between var, let, const

Ans: let is used to declare the variable or initialize a variable.let variable can be updated in future.Its block scope and we cannot redeclare it. let cannot redeclare a value but we can update the value. var is just like let but var is used to declare a variable value at top of the program, its hoisted. var will be redeclared and we can update its value. Initilizing and declaring a variable at a time. const should be declared and intialized. Cannot redeclare the variable again.

Ans: It's a shorter syntax and thus requires less code. Arrow functions are best for callbacks or methods like map , reduce , or forEach. Regular functions created using function declarations or expressions are constructible and callable.
